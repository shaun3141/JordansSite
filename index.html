ABOUT THE BAND

Jordan Willis began his dance career at his high school at the age of 16. He started the first All-Boy's Dance Team at Roosevelt High School. The team went on to place in the top 3 and first in every competition they entered during the dance team's first year. Jordan knew dance was definitely something he would love and cherish for the.rest of his life. Jordan joined DMI Dance Force with his good friend Tyler Foote. Tyler and DMI introduced him to a new world!!!!! Jordan hia trained all over - Millennium Dance Complex, Debbie Reynolds, Iowa Dance Academy, and Edge Dance Center in Los Angeles, California. He has also danced with a lot.of amazing dancers and choreographers! Lesle Scott, KenyaClay (ABDC), Mykell Wilson, Flavia Scheizwer (WYTYCD, Switzerland), .Jaquel knight (Beyonce, Monsters of Hip Hop), Matt Stefanina, and Ava 3. Berne are some of famous dancers/choreographers who have really inspired Jordan.

While dance is a great passion, it is not his only one! Jordan is a singer/actor/model. While living in Los Angeles, he was in a few shows that are popular today: "Glee," "Shake It Up," "No Ordinary Family," etc. Jordan, by all means, just LOVES to entertain.
